Meet the Founder

Olatunde Philomena Itohan

With over 10 years of experience in customer relations and sales management, Itohan is passionate about community development and healthcare accessibility.

She holds a BSc in Geology and Mineral Science from the University of Ilorin and an MSc in Petroleum Geology from the University of Benin.

Through IPF, she is committed to helping individuals living with kidney disease access life-saving dialysis treatment.
